News 1

Financial news is any news that pertains to money and investments, including news on markets. Financial news can be presented in many formats, including articles, videos, tables, charts, podcasts, broadcasts and more, and can encapsulate broad topics that affect the finance industry, such as economic policies, other legislation and major global news events.

For individuals, financial news is beneficial because it provides information that can affect their personal finances and investment portfolio. Financial news is essential to financial professionals, as it can have a positive,negative and neutral effect on investing.
